My Favorite Movies

Explore my favorite films across different genres!

Comedy

The Hangover

Director: Todd Phillips

Stars: Bradley Cooper, Zach Galifianakis, Ed Helms

Three buddies wake up from a bachelor party in Las Vegas, with no memory of the previous night and the bachelor missing.

Bridesmaids

Director: Paul Feig

Stars: Kristen Wiig, Maya Rudolph, Rose Byrne

Competition between the maid of honor and a bridesmaid threatens to upend the life of an out-of-work pastry chef.

Romance

La La Land

Director: Damien Chazelle

Stars: Ryan Gosling, Emma Stone

A jazz pianist and an aspiring actress fall in love while pursuing their dreams in Los Angeles.

The Notebook

Director: Nick Cassavetes

Stars: Ryan Gosling, Rachel McAdams

A poor yet passionate young man falls in love with a rich young woman, giving her a sense of freedom.

Action

Inception

Director: Christopher Nolan

Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt

A thief who steals corporate secrets through dream-sharing technology is given the inverse task of planting an idea into the mind of a C.E.O.

John Wick

Director: Chad Stahelski

Stars: Keanu Reeves, Michael Nyqvist

An ex-hit-man comes out of retirement to track down the gangsters that killed his dog and took everything from him.
